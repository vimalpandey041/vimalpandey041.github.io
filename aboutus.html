About The School

Delhi Public School, Mahendragarh is under the auspices of Delhi Public School Society, New Delhi. DPS Society is one of the leaders in the academic world and is well known for its progressive approach and commitment in academic excellence throughout India and other countries.

DPS Mahendragarh with its well defined teaching methodology and infrastructure is preparing students for the Central Board of Secondary Education (CBSE), New Delhi. The school offers schooling from Nursery to Class XII.

Mission Statement

The Schools of the DPS Society are centres of education where:
  • We aim to preserve and enhance the highest standards of excellence and prepare the students to be part of an enlightened Society of the 21st century.
  • DPS Mahendragarh students are helped to master the expanding horizons of technology and also develop a deep commitment to Indian ethos and culture in its pristine form.
  • DPS Mahendragarh students are equipped to access the best opportunities of growth and advancement in every field as they are fully trained linguistically and culturally. The concept of enlightened global citizens will be the backdrop of all their endeavors.
  • DPS Mahendragarh Students are carefully sensitized to environmental concerns, the feeling of nationalism and communal harmony.
  • We understand the contemporary challenges in education and commit to fulfilling collective responsibilities towards children in the country.
